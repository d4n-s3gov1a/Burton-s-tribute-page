Tim Burton

In horror we trust.

Timothy Walter Burton is an American film director, producer, writer and artist. He is known for his gothic fantasy and horror films, such as:

  • (1985) Pee-wee's Big Adventure
  • (1988) Beetlejuice
  • (1989) Batman
  • (1990) Edward Scissorhands
  • (1992) Batman Returns
  • (1996) Mars Attacks!
  • (1999) Sleepy Hollow
  • (2005) Charlie and the Chocolate Factory
  • (2005) The Corpse Bride
  • (2012) Frankenweenie
